Overview

Alex, Provata CEO, got in touch with me regarding building the user interface for their HRA in October 2016. I was building the new Smashing Magazine at the time, so we decided to get started with the project in January 2017.

I had worked with Alex and his team two times before, building the Provata marketing Web site in 2015 and 2016. So it didn’t take long for the project to get kickstarted. Everything was just as smooth as ever.

And as with our previous collaboration, the team had all the content for the UI ready before-hand, along with the design and the interaction requirements.
